WARZONE CAMPAIGN DESIGNS
OBJECTIVE

To appeal to female, male, and non-binary beauty guru gamers as well as those in the gaming community.

KEY INSIGHTS

Untapped Market- COD is the 2nd most popular video game among female gamers (Statista).

Taking it Global- Extending Call of Duty beyond just the game allows room to reach a global audience rather than just previous gamers.

Mainstream Culture-Introducing a popular game like Call of Duty as a makeup brand in a major store like Ulta, will be shocking but keep the media talking.

Incentives-Codes will encourage new players to try Warzone and recurring players to buy the products for themselves or their significant other.

BIG IDEA

Introducing a Call of Duty makeup line which brings the world of beauty into the gaming universe.

Introduce a Warpaint line to compliment Call of Duty Warzone.

Each product contains a code to unlock exclusive new skins in the game.

Beauty shoppers will be able to experience Warzone at in-store gaming stations.

TAKEAWAYS

Female gamers are a large, relatively untapped market when it comes to First Person Shooters such as COD.

The beauty industry is a large and successful market with a diverse group of individuals.

Our collaboration with Nyx will attract new players to Warzone and reignite attention and love for the game.

ROLE

Research target audience's needs and wants in the world of beauty and gaming.

Design mockups in photoshop and indesign to make makeup products that tie into the campaign, as well as design new skins as incentives for gamers.

COORS LIGHT CAMPAIGN DESIGNS
STRATEGY

To inspire 21-27 year-olds to put down their phones and seek time to unwind and refresh

BIG IDEA

To incorporate the Coors Light brand into the digital sphere and provide a call to action to unplug and unwind.

TAKEAWAYS

Our target audience is a young group age 21-27 and we thought the best way to reach them would be through digital ads that you would see on your phone.

Our idea of unplugging and unwinding plays along with the theme of the cell phone because we are constantly looking at our phones throughout the day.

So what better way to "unwind" after a long day than by "unplugging" our phones and relaxing with an ice-cold Coors Light.

ROLE

Research specific cell phone usage stats to get a better understanding of our target audience and how they enjoy spending their free time.

Design the logo that appears on the static ads, as well as create the logo on the wrist band for a college event that will get people off their phone and living in the moment.

BASKIN ROBBINS CAMPAIGN DESIGNS
OBJECTIVE

The main objective of our campaign is to show that all parents can utilize Baskin Robbins as a reward for their children while also getting a reward for themselves. As a "household negotiator" and a guardian to children, parents, grandparents, and babysitters know the importance of pleasing their young ones. Baskin Robbins' main competition is grocery stores frozen aisles, so with our ads we hope to promote the loving bond between guardian and child that gets everyone up and outside the house for a real ice cream experience.

KEY INSIGHTS

Baskin Robbins offers 31 unique flavors in stores at all times, so anybody can find what they are looking for.

TAKEAWAYS

To offer a more special experience with loved ones that grocery store ice cream cannot.

ROLE

To research our target audience and come up with a strategic campaign to help the "household negotioator" reward their young ones.

To design billboard, bus stop, and digital ads that were bright and eye catching to get our target audience's attention.
